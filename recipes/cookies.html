HOW

You'll need:

  • 4 TBS unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 TBS cream cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar, plus extra for rolling
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup + 2 TBS all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp fine sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F, and line a standard-sized sheet pan with parchment paper. Gather ingredients.
  2. In a medium bowl, beat with an electric the melted butter, cream cheese, granulated sugar and vanilla extract until homogenous and fluffy.
  3. Next, sprinkle the flour, baking soda and sea salt on top. Beat in just to combine; do not over mix.
  4. Divide the mixture into 6-7 dough balls, and roll into balls.
  5. Roll each ball in extra granulated sugar before placing evenly spaced on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10-11 minutes. They will be puffy when you remove them from the oven and start to crack as they cool. Let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before moving to a wire rack to cool completely.
